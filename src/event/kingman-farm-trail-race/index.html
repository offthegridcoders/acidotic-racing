course details

THERE IS NO DAY OF EVENT ENTRY DUE TO THE LIMITED PARKING.

We're getting back to our roots a little (pun intended) with one of the newest editions to our renowned event schedule. The Kingman Farm Trail Race will be an old-school show up, lace up, and race sort of event. We've designed a course that's both beginner friendly and challenging at the same time. Packed into roughly 3.6 miles you'll encounter nordic-type paths, switchback single-track, and corn fields. Follow it up with some killer prizes from our generous sponsors and a homemade baked good or two and we promise you'll put this event on your schedule every year.
