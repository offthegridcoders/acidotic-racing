details

We will host a FREE product demo and course preview. Light refreshments and prizes after the run.

All competitors will start at the same time in the same location. At a predetermined spot the 10 mile & 4.6 mile races will split with competitors going in different directions. This a very technical trail race. There is arguably no better urban trail running and mountain biking network in the northeast than the Oaklands & Henderson-Swasey Town Forests. Located just minutes from downtown Exeter, NH these trails are better known as Fort Rock. If it's technical trail running you are looking for...search no further. This may be the most technical trail race anywhere in New England!
